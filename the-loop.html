Four tools do the grinding.

The loop runs on four things you can see in every deal. None of them ask you to read the twenty-page report — they surface the delta, the gap, the number, and the call.

Version history

See what changed, not just what’s there.

Every re-run diffs against the last version: what got revised, what’s new, which concerns appeared. Six weeks of a deal, readable in one panel.

Open concerns

The AI names what to find next.

Each gap is ranked by severity and typed (data gap, weak value, open question), and most clear with a single artifact. Your discovery list writes itself.

Report # Dashboard

Key numbers, not twenty pages.

The cards carry the signal — value, trend, confidence, source. Catch the movement and the key indications in seconds; open the full report only when you need to defend one.

Decision → Radar

Decide, then let it watch.

Go, No-Go, or Conditional. A Conditional Go hands its open conditions to a standing radar that auto-flags the moment one slips, so a “maybe” never quietly rots.
