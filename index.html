Questions partners ask

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT answers prompts; answers die in a chat history. MindLab is an installed workflow: drafts are structured to your templates, carry sources, pass through review, and become firm records you can reuse.

How is this different from AlphaSense or Hebbia?

Those platforms sell content breadth and automation. MindLab is built around what happens between AI output and your firm’s record: the approval gate, the audit trail, and compounding memory of your own work.

Is our data used to train models?

No. Your workspace is isolated and your data serves only your firm.

Where does our data live?

In your private workspace; export and deletion on request. Deployment specifics are covered in the security documentation.

What exactly happens in the pilot?

We map one workflow, configure your templates, run live deals through it with your team for 30–90 days, then you decide whether it becomes your system.

Can it match our memo format?

Yes. That calibration is most of what the pilot is.

What if we leave?

You export everything. The records are yours.
